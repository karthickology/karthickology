Note-Taking Tips

Effective note-taking can significantly improve your learning:

  • Use your own words to summarize concepts
  • Organize notes with headings and bullet points
  • Highlight key terms and definitions
  • Review and revise notes regularly
  • Use visual aids like diagrams when helpful
